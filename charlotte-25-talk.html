  • Noé Cuneo and Renaud Raquépas.
    Large deviations of return times and related entropy estimators on shift spaces.
    Communications in Mathematical Physics 405 (2024).

  • Noé Cuneo, Vojkan Jakšić, Claude-Alain Pillet and Armen Shirikyan
