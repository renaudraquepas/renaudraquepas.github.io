Talk at the IEEE Int. Symp. Inf. Theory 2024, in Athens

Ziv–Merhav estimation for hidden-Markov processes

We present a proof of strong consistency of a Zi–Merhav-type estimator of the cross entropy rate for pairs of hidden-Markov processes. Our proof strategy has two novel aspects: the focus on decoupling properties of the laws and the use of tools from the thermodynamic formalism.
